The green house is Morrigan's personal estate.
It is a green tinted glass pyramid with 5 floors, it has entrances from all 4 sides, all on the 3rd floor. It is powered by its own generators located on the 1st floor, powered by organic matter. The house is a decorative wonder, filled with organic life artworks created and gathered by Morrigan from her 30,000 years of traveling.
