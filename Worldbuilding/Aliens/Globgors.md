There is a music master that knows all instruments, but there is only 1 such Globgor alive at once, and there is a council of music that predicts and trains the next Music Master every 100 years. They say that the music master can produce all music at once, and they can control all organisms with their music, for the purpose of having fun and peace.  
These Globgors exist on multiple planets throughout the galaxy of JXJ, which has around 106 planets and moons. It is suspected that Globgors used to be a domineering space-tech civilization that built a dyson swarm. But nuked themselves back to the tribal age. Some of their tech can still be found on their planets. Gems came along after Globgors nuked each other out, they took the dyson swarm for themselves, and took over the solar system and enslaved the Globgors in it. Some managed to escape.
