#Gems

**Corvette** - Smallest kind of ship, fast and nimble, designed for escort and scout. Fit around 10 people. Often fit in larger ships as escape ships or fighter ships.  
**Frigate** - Larger than Corvettes, also for escorting, but larger and is used for tanking and protecting convoys and trade ships. Fits around 25 people.  
**Destroyer** - Larger than Frigates, made for escorting important ships and cruisers. Fits around 100 people.  
**Hand/Foot Ship** - General purpose ship that is often used by middle class gems to conduct missions. Has defensive capabilities, amenities and prisons. Fits around 150 people.  
**Cruiser** - Classic military ship for warring and raiding, is tanky, has a lot of offensive capabilities. Fits around 300 people.  
**Arm/Leg Ship** - Elite ships used by the elite gems, more prestigious and larger version of the hand/foot ship. Fits around 500 people.  
**Battleship** - Largest kind of military ship, has the deadliest and most amount of guns, is so valuable that is not lightly thrown around in raids, and is only present in full on invasions and battles. Fits around 800 people.  
**Diamond Ships** - 4 Ships made for the diamonds that can fuse together into a large voltron. Each ship fits around 500 people for 2000 in total when combined.
