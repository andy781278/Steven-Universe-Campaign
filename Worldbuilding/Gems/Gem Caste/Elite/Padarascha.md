#Gems/Type #Gems/Elite #Gems/Yellow
Padparadscha is an overseer/advisor that possesses incredible insight and wisdom into current situations. They're made for advising the diamonds and other elites, and usually oversees some territories as well.

Padparadscha is a Gem with the same height and a very similar appearance to Sapphire. She has a coral orange complexion, plump lips, and shoulder-length, pale peach hair, with bangs covering her eye and a cowlick on top of her head. Her attire consists of an off-white, peach, and reddish orange floor-length ruffled gown, with an off-white collar, shoulder pads, and elbow-length gloves. The skirt is peach, and it has an reddish orange upper layer with an off-white trim and is tied back into a three-notched bow.
