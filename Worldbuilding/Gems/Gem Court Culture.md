#Gems/Society

White Diamond

- Hive Mind: Every gem in WD’s court is connected to WD, she can hear everyone’s thoughts, and can micro-manage all of their activities simultaneously. As a result, her court is extremely organized and efficient, like robots.
- Court Gems: Goshenite, Bornite, Nacre.
- Gems: Opal, Albite, Selenite, Rainbow Quartz, Desert Glass, Dolomite, Bismuth

Yellow Diamond

- Blunt: Aggressive, straight to the point, sometimes almost carelessly so.
- Court Gems: Emerald, Heliodor, Morganite, Hessonite, Emerald, Demantoid, Mali, Rhodizite, Sunstone
- Gems: Nephrite, Topaz, Jasper, Carnelian, Ruby, Aragonite, Peridot, Pyrite.

Blue Diamond

- Egotistical: Since most gems in blue’s court are diplomatic rather than soldiers, they view senseless violence as barbaric and look down upon most other lesser gems.
- Court Gems: Aquamarine, Maxixe, Sapphire, Apatite, Lapis, Amazonite,
- Gems: Hollyblue Agate, Zircon, Tanzanite, Cobalt, Alexandrite, Larimar.

Pink Diamond

- Young: Regionalized almost entirely on Earth, Pink does not have many gems, and since she is also bad at ruling, their gems are childish, unruly, and chaotic.
- Court Gems: Bixbite, Rhodolite
- Gems: Jasper, Amethyst, Rose Quartz, Spinel, Petalite.
