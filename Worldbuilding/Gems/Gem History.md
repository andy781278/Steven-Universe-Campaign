#Gems

- ~3 billion years ago: Andromeda Galaxy forms through a major merger.
- ~1.5 billion years ago: The Solar System Mayall II forms along with Xypha and its planets, including Homeworld.
- ~300 million years ago: The first pebble lifeforms appeared on Homeworld.
- ~100 million years ago: The first neo-pebbles appear.
- ~10 million years ago: The first proto-gems appear
- ~800,000 years ago: The proto-gems dominate the planet.
- ~30,000 years ago: The first modern gem: White diamond, is born as a result of Fracturation of a parent proto-gem. Her design is so flawless that she managed to dominate the planet within a few decades. She invented a new reproduction method by draining the resources of the planet.
- ~29,000 years ago: Yellow and Blue was born, and was tasked with expansion.
- ~28,000 years ago: Pink diamond was born.
