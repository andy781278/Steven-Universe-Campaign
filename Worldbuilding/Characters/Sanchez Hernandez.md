#### History
- Sanchez was born without a father, and his mother died when he was 3.
- As an orphan, he roamed around the streets of Catan, stealing and fighting, lost a leg, got a new one.
- He learned to scheme and schemed his way up a pyramid scheme, and got rich.
- Got caught and sentenced to 20 years in jail, broke out after 8 years and on the run
- Founded Chill Dudaz with Mech Man.
- Met a lovely lady Auspicious Tingo and married her.
- Bis killed Auspicious Tingo, driving Sanchez into rage and started a gang warfare with Morrigon
