#### History
- Real name: **Tree**
- Tree was born and raised in Polymoria
- Chrys used to punch tree day and night, it was his best friend.
- There was also a tree that fell on him one time, and it hurt.
- The tree is angry for all those years of being punched, so after Chrys left, the tree plots revenge for years.
- They managed to study cybernetics and became a cyborg by controlling a mech with himself in the center, controlling it by excreting the necessary juices at the right spot.
- They went on to Catan and started a gang called the Chill Dudas along with Sanchez Hernandez.
